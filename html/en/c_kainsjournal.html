Kain's Journal: Thorn Pages

2010-2011, Warcraft 3 TFT mod

This project was one of my first modding experiences and a massive level-design task. A middle-sized map in Warcraft 3 - The Frozen Throne is an homage to the Diablo game series. Throughout the years of development the ideas for this project have changed a lot, but the basics can be described like this: "Large banquet spaces and claustrophobic hallways, vast ancient library and a brief glance at the barren nature outside. The setting is grim and covered in thick mist. However, even in these dimly-lit chambers there is light seeping through the cracks and windows of the old cathedral, giving the ever-so-slight hope before the gates of hell."

Warcraft 3 modding was an arduous path at first. The game engine and its built-in editor had a few limitations. In addition, the use of in-game assets had to be creative in order to impress the players. But thanks to a welcoming modding community and a lot of related articles, I've learned and tried to apply the knowledge in Kain's Journal: Thorn Pages. The map has gone through several overhauls and took almost 2 years to complete. By the end of development besides the map layout itself I had detailed descriptions of hero-classes, enemies and in-game abilities as well as item- and monster-rarity specifications.
