Libraries’ News

2014, book cover design

Another job for Vologda Tendryakov Library. This time it was an analytics book about how the libraries in Vologda region work with youth. The cover was designed to look like a newspaper front page with outtakes that resemble articles. This cover turned out nicely and both the client and I enjoyed the final product.
