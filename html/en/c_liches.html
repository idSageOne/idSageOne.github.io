Liches

Winter 2015-2016, card game

Liches is the result of my work in GameMaker: Studio. It is a card game, where every card buffs a hero instead of cards fighting among themselves. Even though a lot of features of modern card games (e.g. network multiplayer) are not present, I still consider it a huge personal success. The game had a set amount of features planned and all of them were finished. The development process went smoothly and it took roughly 2 months to finish the game.

In addition it was the first time, when I had to support a released product. In my future projects I decided to take player feedback into better consideration, pay more attention to the interface simplicity and usability. I have also learned the benefits of beta-testing and PR on social media, none of which I’ve done before. Regardless of the project’s small scale, I’ve learned a lot throughout the development process.
