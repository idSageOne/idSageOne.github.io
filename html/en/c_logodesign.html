Game Boom Contest

2013, logo design

I have designed a logo for a game development contest hosted by XGM Community. The idea was simple: a controller to symbolize gaming, conductive tracks to symbolize development, contest name and the site logo on one of the controller buttons. The work process was fairly simple and self-explanatory.

Young Librarian Meet-up

2014, logo design

Young librarian meet-up logo was made for Vologda Tendryakov Library as a part of books for youth initiative. The design was left completely to my imagination, so I went with simple geometry, materials and vibrant colors. The other designs consisted of even simpler forms and the library has chosen the “owl” variation, which they are still using to this day.
