XGM Community achievements

2014, graphic design

In 2014 the modding site, I was a part of has changed their design, so I decided to throw in some help by drawing the achievement icons. The achievements included personal blog posts, chat activity, years on site and some others.

The work process consisted of outlining, coloring and then adding effects and filters. On the side note all drawing was done with a mouse and a brush tool, sacrificing line precision and smoothness for drawing speed. In the end, the site administration chose not to use these icons and went with the already existing design.
